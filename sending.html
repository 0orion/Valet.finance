Step 5: Send Bitcoin

You can send Bitcoin in two ways: on-chain or through the Lightning Network. Choose the method that best suits your needs.

Sending On-Chain Bitcoin

  1. From the home screen, tap on the "Send" tab at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Enter the recipient's Bitcoin address or scan their QR code.
  3. Enter the amount you want to send (in BTC or your local currency).
  4. Review the transaction fee and total amount.
  5. Add a description (optional).
  6. Confirm the transaction.
Tip: Double-check the recipient's address before sending. Bitcoin transactions cannot be reversed.

Sending Lightning Payments

  1. From the home screen, tap on the "Lightning" tab.
  2. Tap on "Send" or the "-" button.
  3. Enter the Lightning invoice or scan the QR code.
  4. Review the payment details.
  5. Confirm the payment.

Transaction Fees

  • On-chain: Fees vary based on network congestion. Higher fees mean faster confirmation.
  • Lightning: Minimal fees, typically less than 1 satoshi.

Transaction Status

  • Pending: The transaction is being processed
  • Confirmed: The transaction has been added to the blockchain
  • Failed: The transaction could not be completed
Important: Always verify the amount and recipient before confirming any transaction.

Transaction History

You can view your transaction history in the "Transactions" tab:

  • See all your sent and received payments
  • View transaction details and status
  • Export transaction history for record-keeping
