Wallet Configuration

Access the settings menu by tapping the gear icon in the top-right corner of the home screen.

Security Settings

  • PIN Protection: Set up a PIN to secure your wallet
  • Biometric Authentication: Enable fingerprint or face recognition (if available)
  • Auto-lock: Set how quickly the wallet locks when inactive

Display Settings

  • Currency: Choose your preferred fiat currency for price display
  • Theme: Switch between light and dark mode
  • Language: Select your preferred language

Network Settings

  • Node Selection: Choose your preferred Lightning node
  • Fee Rate: Set default transaction fee rates
  • Network Mode: Switch between mainnet and testnet
Tip: Keep your app updated to the latest version for the best security and features.

Backup & Recovery

  • View Recovery Phrase: Access your 12-word backup phrase
  • Export Wallet: Create a backup of your wallet data
  • Import Wallet: Restore from a backup
Important: Never share your recovery phrase or wallet backup with anyone.

Advanced Settings

  • Transaction History: View and export your transaction history
  • Channel Management: Monitor and manage Lightning channels
  • Debug Information: Access technical details for troubleshooting

Support & Help

  • FAQ: Access frequently asked questions
  • Contact Support: Get help from the Valet team
  • About: View app version and legal information

Local Channel Backup

This is always automatically set to "Enabled"; however, if it is disabled, kindly set it to "Enabled". This backup helps restore your Lightning Channel states if you uninstall your wallet or lose your device.

Backup Storage Location

This is always set to your download folder. It is important to assign this to any folder of your choice that you'll remember, because this is where the wallet backup files will be saved, and it'd be handy when you next want to recover your wallet on your mobile.

Chain Wallet Management

This is automatically set to "BIP84 Modern Wallet". You can leave it there.

Hardware Wallet Integration

Hardware wallets are physical Bitcoin wallets that may exist as a USB drive or specialized devices designed to store Bitcoin. If you have one, you can link these devices to your Valet wallet in a Watch-Only mode, creating an extra layer of safety. Just scan the wallet and follow the prompts.

Electrum Node Configuration

By default, your wallet will be connected to random Electrum nodes, but through this setting, you can connect to private Electrum servers (if you have any) for easy on-chain Bitcoin blockchain access. Electrum servers are servers that intermediate between Electrum wallets and the Bitcoin network, removing the hurdles of downloading and syncing the full blockchain.

Fiat Currency Display

This is the local currency your balance is shown. It is set to USD by default. However, Valet supports a few popular local currencies like:

  • US Dollar (USD)
  • British Pound Sterling (GBP)
  • Euro (EUR)
  • Japanese Yen (JPY)
  • And many others
Tip: Choose your preferred currency to make it easier to understand your Bitcoin balance in terms of your local currency.
