Step 4: Receive Bitcoin

There are two ways to receive Bitcoin in your Valet wallet: on-chain and through the Lightning Network.

Receiving On-Chain Bitcoin

  1. From the home screen, tap on the "Receive" tab at the bottom of the screen.
  2. You'll see your Bitcoin address. You can share this address with anyone who wants to send you Bitcoin.
  3. Tap on the address to copy it or use the share button to send it via other apps.
Tip: Always verify the address before sharing it. Bitcoin transactions cannot be reversed.

Receiving Lightning Payments

  1. From the home screen, tap on the "Lightning" tab.
  2. Tap on "Receive" or the "+" button.
  3. Enter the amount you want to receive (in BTC or your local currency).
  4. Add a description (optional).
  5. Share the generated Lightning invoice with the sender.

Payment Notifications

When you receive a payment:

  • You'll see a notification on your device
  • The payment will appear in your transaction history
  • Your balance will update automatically

Payment Status

  • Pending: The payment is being processed
  • Completed: The payment has been received
  • Failed: The payment could not be completed
Important: Lightning invoices expire after a certain time. Make sure to share them quickly with the sender.
